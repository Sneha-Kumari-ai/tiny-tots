Learning About Seasons

Spring

Spring is the season of growth and renewal. Flowers bloom, animals wake from hibernation, and the weather starts to warm up.

Summer

Summer is the warmest season of the year. Days are longer, and people often go on vacation to enjoy the sunny weather, beaches, and outdoor activities.
